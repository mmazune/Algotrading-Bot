## FILES CREATED

### 1. axfl/strategies/session_breakout.py (104 lines)
- UTC session window tracking [start_hhmm, end_hhmm]
- Per-day high/low detection with cumulative tracking
- Post-session breakout detection with buffer (pips)
- ATR-based SL/TP (default 1.4 ATR SL, 2.0 ATR TP)
- Minimum range filter (6.0 pips default)
- Default: Asian session 00:00-05:00 UTC

### 2. axfl/strategies/vol_contraction.py (68 lines)
- ATR percentile contraction detection (bottom 30% of 100-bar window)
- Short lookback range (10 bars) for micro-range identification
- Breakout of tight range with buffer (1.0 pip)
- ATR-based SL/TP (1.2 ATR SL, 1.8 ATR TP)
- Minimum range filter (4.0 pips)
